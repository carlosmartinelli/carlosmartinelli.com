About Me

Turning Ideas into
Digital Magic for Everyone

Carlos Martinelli is a proficient UI Developer currently affiliated with OHO Interactive. He leverages his robust background in software engineering and web development to create cutting-edge, user-focused solutions. Possessing a Master's degree in Business Administration from Cambridge College, Carlos seamlessly integrates technical aptitude with business-centric strategies, driving optimized outcomes across various roles.

His professional journey traces back to Maringá, Brazil, where he cultivated his foundational technical skills. Gradually, Carlos assumed greater responsibilities, managing intricate web development projects that honed his leadership and project management skills. Embracing the challenges of independent work, he transitioned into freelancing, where he expanded his professional horizons by catering to a diverse clientele based in the United States, particularly in Massachusetts.

Carlos' career is marked by his adaptability and commitment to growth, continually improving his craft and adapting to ever-evolving tech trends. His blend of tech-savvy expertise and strategic business insight positions him as a key asset in any organization or project.
